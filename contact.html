Fun Facts About Birds

  • Birds are the direct descendants of dinosaurs.
  • Some species of birds live upwards of 60 years.
  • Parrots are known for their dancing antics. They make deliberate movements that they adjust with changes in tempo and rhythm.
  • Birds are sensitive and experience a broad range of emotions.
  • Parrots can learn words and phrases, with some developing a vocabulary of over 1,000 words and phrases.
  • African greys have shown the ability to use words and phrases in the proper context to allow for verbal communication, taking their use of language beyond mimicry.
  • Some species of birds have shown advanced abilities including the use of tools and the ability to solve puzzles.
